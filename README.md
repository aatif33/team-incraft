Official website of **Team INCRAFT**, the international student engineering team from **Kumaraguru Institutions**, proudly representing **India** at the **Europe Submarine Challenge 2026**.

---

## 🚀 About Team INCRAFT

Team INCRAFT is a multidisciplinary student engineering team dedicated to designing, developing, and building **TiMi**, a next-generation human-powered submarine.

Our mission is to advance sustainable marine engineering while representing India on the global stage through innovation, teamwork, and engineering excellence.
