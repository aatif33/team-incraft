TiMi 1.0

Sustainable Innovation Beneath The Surface

TiMi 1.0 integrates lightweight structural engineering, sustainable materials, human-powered propulsion and advanced safety mechanisms into one underwater platform. Every subsystem is engineered by students, from concept to manufacturing.
