TiMi 1.0

Sustainable Innovation Beneath The Surface

TiMi 1.0 integrates lightweight structural engineering, sustainable materials, human-powered propulsion and advanced safety mechanisms into one underwater platform. Every subsystem is engineered by students, from concept to manufacturing.

ENGINEERING DESIGN

Inside TiMi 1.0

Every subsystem is designed, analysed and manufactured by Team INCRAFT to maximize efficiency, sustainability and safety.

CORE FEATURES

Engineered For Performance

Sustainable Hull

Lightweight composite structure developed using sustainable engineering principles for strength, durability and efficiency.

Human Powered

Advanced pedal propulsion system delivering efficient underwater performance with zero-emission operation.

Advanced Safety Lock

Engineered emergency locking and quick-release mechanisms to ensure maximum diver safety.
