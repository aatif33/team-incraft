OUR STORY

From Classroom Ideas To International Innovation

Team INCRAFT was established with the vision of creating world-class marine engineering solutions through student innovation. Bringing together passionate engineers, designers and innovators, the team develops sustainable underwater technologies while proudly representing Kumaraguru Institutions on the global stage.

OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To engineer sustainable, innovative and human-powered underwater technologies while nurturing future engineers capable of solving real-world marine challenges through interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our Vision

To establish Team INCRAFT as one of the world's leading student marine engineering teams by creating innovative submarines that redefine sustainability, safety and engineering excellence.

OUR VALUES

Sustainability
