Our Institution

Kumaraguru Institutions

Kumaraguru Institutions is one of India's leading centres for engineering education, innovation, and entrepreneurship. With world-class laboratories, industry collaborations, and a strong culture of research, it empowers students to transform ideas into impactful engineering solutions.

Our Proud

Kct Garage

KCT Garage serves as Team INCRAFT's engineering workspace, where concepts evolve into functional prototypes. It provides the infrastructure, fabrication facilities, and collaborative environment required to design, manufacture, assemble, and test TiMi.

The Competion

The Europe International Submarine Races

The Europe International Submarine Races (EISR) is one of the world's premier engineering competitions, bringing together university teams to design, build, and race human-powered submarines. Representing India and Kumaraguru Institutions, Team INCRAFT proudly joins this international platform with TiMi, showcasing sustainable engineering, advanced underwater technology.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission

To engineer sustainable, innovative and human-powered underwater technologies while nurturing future engineers capable of solving real-world marine challenges through interdisciplinary collaboration.

OUR VISION

Our Vision

To establish Team INCRAFT as one of the world's leading student marine engineering teams by creating innovative submarines that redefine sustainability, safety and engineering excellence.
